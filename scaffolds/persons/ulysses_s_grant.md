## Analytical Purpose

Grant tests the Union military symbolic world of plainness, work, pressure,
instrumental force, surrender, and settlement. He is an important foil for both
Lincoln and Confederate providential/honor registers.

---

Expanded priors:

Recommended documents:

1. Fort Donelson surrender correspondence, 1862 - official correspondence.
2. Vicksburg campaign reports, 1863 - battle reports.
3. Correspondence with Lincoln, 1864-1865 - official correspondence.
4. Overland Campaign reports, 1864 - battle reports.
5. Appomattox correspondence with Lee, 1865 - official correspondence.
6. General Orders and post-surrender statements, 1865 - official military voice.
7. `Personal Memoirs`, 1885 - retrospective memoir.
